Privacy Policy

Acceptance of Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the use of the Blackhole interface (“Blackhole”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) available at blackhole.exchange. Blackhole is an experimental, community-driven, non-custodial routing interface that enables users to interact with decentralized exchange and bridge protocols. Blackhole is operated on a decentralized basis and is not a registered legal entity.
By using the Platform, you agree to this Policy and our Terms of Use.

/1/ Information We Collect

1.1 Public Blockchain Data The Platform may access and display publicly available blockchain data, such as wallet addresses, token balances, transaction hashes, and chain identifiers. This information is retrieved directly from blockchain nodes and is not stored, collected, or linked to any personal identifiers by Blackhole.

1.2 Usage Analytics We may use third-party analytics tools to collect anonymized and aggregated data for the purpose of improving the Platform. This may include: Device and browser type Page views and interaction flow System performance and load behavior Aggregated usage volumes (non-attributable) This data does not include personal identifiers. You may disable cookies or analytics through your browser settings.

1.3 Cookies and Local Storage We may use strictly necessary cookies or browser storage to remember preferences such as language or selected networks. No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored.

/2/ Use of Information

The information described above is used to:
1. Maintain and improve the functionality of the Platform
2. Monitor usage patterns and identify errors
3. Optimize routing logic and on-chain performance
4. Support non-personalized product development

We do not collect names, emails, passwords, or private keys. All transactions are executed via user-controlled wallets.

/3/ AI and Data Processing

Blackhole may use internal or external AI models for:
1. Route scoring based on Execution Confidence Score (ECS)
2. Transaction risk analysis
3. Gas estimation and latency optimization

These models may access anonymized data only. No personal identifiers are shared with AI providers.

/4/ Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent any user data. Limited non-personal data may be shared with: 1. Infrastructure providers (e.g., RPC, analytics) for platform maintenance
2. AI service providers, strictly for routing and execution optimization
3. Legal authorities if required by applicable law
4. Researchers or developers, only in anonymized form

/5/ Data Retention

We do not store personal data. Aggregated analytics may be stored for up to 12 months for system analysis and debugging purposes. Public blockchain data cannot be deleted or altered.

/6/ User Rights

As a non-custodial interface, you retain full control over your wallet, keys, and on-chain interactions. You may:
1. Disable analytics via browser settings
2. Delete local browser storage manually
3. Contact us for feedback regarding inaccurate on-chain displays
4. Disable AI features, if and when such toggles are made available.

/7/ International Use

Blackhole is accessible globally. Infrastructure and analytics services may operate in jurisdictions such as the European Union, Singapore, and the United States. By using the Platform, you consent to this cross-border data processing.

/8/ Security

We apply reasonable technical safeguards, including HTTPS encryption, limited browser-side storage, and a non-custodial architecture. However, we cannot guarantee the security of third-party infrastructure or the blockchain itself. You are solely responsible for verifying all transactions.

/9/ Limitation of Liability

The Platform is provided on an “as-is” and “as-available” basis for informational and interface purposes only. Blackhole does not provide financial services, does not validate or verify tokens, and does not custody user assets. All actions on the Platform are performed at your own discretion and risk.

/10/ Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Platform indicates acceptance of the revised Policy.
